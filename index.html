LibreTaxi
Русский

Free and open source Uber/Lyft alternative to connect passengers and drivers.

LibreTaxi makes taxi affordable again by completely removing all layers in between. Passengers pay with cash and get connected with drivers directly, where LibreTaxi is a thin layer allowing to negotiate the price before order is confirmed. No registration/approval is required. Less regulations.

Install Source

Free for drivers

We do not charge for using the service. LibreTaxi app is free for drivers and passengers.

Cash only

No credit cards. You'll need cash to pay drivers directly. BitCoin integration is on the way!

Instant activation

Good it or bad - no approval process. Start earning money as a driver in 1 minute!

Negotiate ride price

Win-win for passengers and drivers. Allows drivers to set their own price or offer discount.

All platforms

Works on iOS, Android, and also on desktop or web app. We use Telegram app as our main transport.

True spirit inside

Contains true cyberpunk spirit. Free, open sourced with MIT license, less tracking, less regulations.

Battery friendly

No GPS tracking, friendly for your battery. Lean and minimalistic UI implemented as chat bot application.

Multiple languages

Currently supports English and Russian. Help us translating LibreTaxi to your language.

Hackable!

Get the source, hack and tune it for your city. Add features specific to your region. Written with JavaScript (ES6).
